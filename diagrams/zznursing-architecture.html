zznursing Architecture

Integrated elderly-care platform connecting management operations, family mini-app access, AI health assessment, IoT monitoring, and enterprise care workflows.

Dual Channels

  • - Vue admin serves institutional operators across the full care lifecycle
  • - WeChat mini-app gives family members status queries and appointment flows
  • - Channel authentication splits between JWT admin and mini-app openId flow

Care Operations

  • - Elder, admission, nursing plans, contracts, and facilities share one platform base
  • - AI health assessment turns uploaded PDFs into structured nursing suggestions
  • - IoT ingestion watches wearable and bedside devices for alert conditions

Cloud Integration

  • - Baidu Qianfan powers health scoring and intervention recommendations
  • - Huawei IoTDA and AMQP bridge device telemetry into the rule engine
  • - Aliyun OSS, Redis, and MySQL preserve reports, cache state, and transactional data
